About Bubbles

Bubbles is the cutest and most emotional of the Powerpuff Girls. She is often the shy, paranoid and easily scared member of the trio.

Bubbles' characteristics

  1. She's compassionate.
  2. She's gentle and innocent.
  3. She's possessing a love for animals and her favorite doll, Octi.
